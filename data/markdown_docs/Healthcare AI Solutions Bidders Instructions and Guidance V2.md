OVERVIEW OF THE PROCUREMENT PROCESS AND INSTRUCTIONS TO BIDDERS

Overview

This is a competitive ITT conducted in accordance with the open procedure (e.g. a single-stage competitive tendering procedure without a restriction on who can submit a Bid) procedure under the Procurement Act 2023.

NHS SBS is acting solely in an agency capacity and not as principal. The resulting Framework Agreement will be executed by NHS SBS on behalf of the Approved Organisations, who shall be the principals to the agreement. Any Call-Off Contracts will be entered into directly between the Supplier and the relevant Approved Organisation.

The intention is to create a Open Framework comprising of 8 Lots. There is no limit on the number of suppliers that can be appointed to the Framework where suppliers meet the award criteria of 55%.

Detail of the functionality of the Open Framework can be found in the Open Framework Description Document contained within this ITT.

The Authority will treat all Bidders fairly and equally through this ITT. Equally, Bidders must comply fully with the instructions provided to ensure that a Complaint Bid is submitted. A non-compliant Bid will be excluded from further consideration.
